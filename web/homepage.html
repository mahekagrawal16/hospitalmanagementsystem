How Emergency Support Works

Instant Help When It Matters Most

In a medical emergency, patients are often unable to communicate their condition. DocVisitNow’s Emergency Support feature uses biometric scanning to identify the individual and share critical medical details with hospitals — within seconds.

What Information is Shared?
  • ✔️ Name and age
  • ✔️ Existing health conditions and allergies
  • ✔️ Current medications
  • ✔️ Emergency contact details
How It Works
  1. Users first create their emergency profile by uploading a clear photo and filling in vital information such as name, city, and emergency contact.
  2. In case of an accident or crisis, a bystander can use the biometric scanner to scan the person’s face.
  3. If the system recognizes the person, their emergency details are instantly displayed on screen — allowing others to contact their family or seek help.
Privacy First: Your information is encrypted and shared only during verified emergencies. You stay in control of your data.

This feature ensures you receive the right help, from the right people, at the right time — even if you're unable to speak.
